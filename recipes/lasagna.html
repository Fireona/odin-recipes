World's Best Lasagna

Image of World's Best Lasagna from Allrecipes

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
    1. Meat sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta mixture
    4. Mozzarella slices
    5. Meat sauce
    6. Parmesan cheese
    7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan.
  5. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
  6. Cover lasagna with foil.
  7. Once the oven is preheated, place lasagna in the oven and cook for 25 minutes with the foil on.
  8. After cooking with the foil on for 25 minutes, remove the foil and cook for another 25 minutes.
  9. Remove lasagna from the oven.
  10. Rest for at least 15 minutes.
  11. Serve.
