Simple Pasta by Me

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of pasta
  • 500 Grams of Ground Meat
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 1 Yellow Onion
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic
  • 0.5 Teaspoon of Oregano
  • 3 Sausages
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to rolling boil. Cook your pasta until al dente. Drain and keep a spoonful of pasta water.
  2. Peel, wash, and dice your vegetables.
  3. Slice your sausages.
  4. On high heat, brown your ground meat.
  5. Add in your onions and garlic. Lower heat to prevent burning.
  6. Once onions start turning translucent, add in tomatoes. Add pasta water here and stir. Use your spatula to scrape the bottom.
  7. Add in your oregano and sausages. Mix well.
  8. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Add your pasta here and mix thoroughly.
  10. Remove from stove and rest for 5 minutes. Serve.
