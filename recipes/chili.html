Chili con Carne by me

Ingredients

  • 1 Kilogram of Red Kidney Beans
  • 1-1.5LB Ground Meat
  • 3 Yellow Onions
  • 4 Stalks of Celery
  • 4 Tomatoes
  • Tomato Paste
  • 1 Litre Beef Broth
  • Cumin
  • Chili Powder
  • Optional: Half a garlic

Steps

  1. Prepare your kidney beans. Soak overnight or for several hours on low heat until soft.
  2. Wash and dice your vegetables.
    • Onions
    • Celery
    • Tomatoes
    • Optional: Garlic
  3. Cook your kidney beans on a rolling boil for 15 minutes. Lower heat to a simmer for 1 hour. Drain and rinse afterwards.
  4. On high heat, brown your ground meat.
  5. Once your ground meat is cooked through and browned, add your onions and garlic here. Mix well to prevent onions from burning.
  6. Once the onions start to clear, add in the celery and tomatoes.
  7. Add in your beef broth and stir well.
  8. Add in your kidney beans into the pot. Add in additional beef broth here to cover the mixture, or use water for the healthier option.
  9. Add in 3 tablespoons of chili powder and 2 tablespoon of cumin. Also add a 2 tablespoons of tomato paste here. Mix well and add salt to taste.
  10. Continue to cook and stir for 30 minutes on a simmer.
  11. Rest for 15 minutes and serve.
